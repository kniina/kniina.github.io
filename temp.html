Maximum Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0° latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the Northern Hemisphere (above 0° latitude) tends to be warmer this time of year than the Southern Hemisphere (below 0° latitude). This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of year this data was gathered. As a matter of fact, during summer the Northern Hemisphere is tilted towards the sun, which means that the sun's rays hit that part of the earth more directly.
